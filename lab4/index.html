How to cook rice

  • Step 1 - Wash rice using a strainer
  • Step 2 - Mince garlic
  • Step 3 - Sear the garlic in the rice cooker with some oil
  • Step 4 - Add water (a little bit more than amount of rice)
  • Step 5 - Add rice, set rice cooker to cook, and wait
  • Step 6 - Enjoy!
